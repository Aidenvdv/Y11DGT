Bloodborne is a game released on March 24, 2015, heavily inspired by the Lovecraftian horror of American author H.P. Lovecraft. Developed by FromSoftware, the studio behind Demon's Souls and the Dark Souls series, Bloodborne is renowned for its challenging gameplay and intricate, subtly-told story.

The game's narrative isn't presented explicitly - players must piece together the story through environmental clues, item descriptions, and careful observation of the game world.
